About me

I write about data, logic, code, and philosophy.

Hi! I’m Piotr Urbańczyk — a computer scientist by training, with a background in data science, cognitive science, and philosophy and I often find myself at the crossroads of these fields.

Since 2009 I’ve been closely involved with the Copernicus Center for Interdisciplinary Studies (now at the Jagiellonian University ). More recently, I’ve worked with the AGH University of Krakow’s Center for Security Technologies , contributing to software used by state security institutions.

This blog is where I share the notes, small experiments, careful explanations, and the kind of reasoning I wish I had encountered earlier.
